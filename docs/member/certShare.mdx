## QRCode Share

From the Member Profile page, under the **Courses** tab you are able to share your course certifications by generating a QRCode.

**Steps**
1. Click the Share Icon Button at the top right of the member courses table. <Icon size={1} path={mdiShareVariant} />

<div style={{display: 'flex', justifyContent: 'center'}}>
<img src={useBaseUrl('img/member-profile-course-table-buttons.png')} alt="member course table buttons" />
</div>

2. Select the Courses you want to share.

:::caution
You cannot share expired courses
:::

<img src={useBaseUrl('img/qrcode-course-select.png')} alt="qrcode course select" />

3. Generate the QRCode and link. <Icon size={1} path={mdiQrcode} />


4. Scan the QRCode or share the link with:
- copying the share table link
- go directly to the share table
- emailing the link
<div style={{display: 'flex', justifyContent: 'center'}}>
<img src={useBaseUrl('img/qrcode-dialog.png')} alt="qrcode dialog" />
</div>

5. This will direct whoever you shared the link with to the [Certifcate Share Table](#certificate-share-table)

### Certificate Share Table
The Certificate Share Table page will look similar to the following;

At the top will be your organization's letter head, with a table showing the selected courses to share underneath.

There are options to:
- <Icon size={1} path={mdiCloudDownload} /> download a csv
- <Icon size={1} path={mdiPrinter} /> print the shared course certifications

<div style={{display: 'flex', justifyContent: 'center'}}>
<img src={useBaseUrl('img/qrcode-share-page.png')} alt="qrcode share table" />
</div>


## Print Proof of Training

There is also the option to print off selected course certification *without* the need to generate a QRCode. This is useful for those without a data plan on their cell phones.

**Steps**

1. Select the Printer icon button. <Icon size={1} path={mdiPrinter} />

<div style={{display: 'flex', justifyContent: 'center', marginBottom: '1rem'}}>
<img src={useBaseUrl('img/member-profile-course-table-buttons.png')} alt="member course table buttons" />
</div>

2. Select the courses you want to share.

<div style={{display: 'flex', justifyContent: 'center', marginBottom: '1rem'}}>
<img src={useBaseUrl('img/cert-print-select.png')} alt="print course select" />
</div>

3. Print off Proof of Training.

<div style={{display: 'flex', justifyContent: 'center', marginBottom: '1rem'}}>
<img src={useBaseUrl('img/proof-of-training.png')} alt="print course select" />
</div>

:::info
The page printed is the same as if you were to use the QRCode share option and use the print functionality on the [Certificate Share Table](#certificate-share-table)
:::
